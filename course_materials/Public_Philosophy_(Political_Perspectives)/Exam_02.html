Exam for Lecture 02

Answer keys

What is the State of Nature? Choose all answer that are TRUE.

The state of nature refers to a situation where there is no government authority or political power.
The state of nature is a thought experiment used to normatively justify the state.
Pick an INCORRECT statement of Hobbes's state of nature.

According to Hobbes, human intelligence will lead people to naturally advance science and technology.
What is an INCORRECT statement of Hobbes's view of human nature?

According to Hobbes, the human mind is immaterial that is independent of the laws of physics.
Choose all INCORRECT statements.

According to Hobbes, the origins of all thought are "senses" that are generated internally independent of external objects.
Pick all statements that are FALSE.

According to Hobbes, only physical forms of power can be counted as true "power."
For Hobbes, sense and memory requires experience.
Choose every INCORRECT statement.

For Hobbes, voluntary motion does not need to rely on thought or imagination.
For Hobbes, anything that is good is objective and absolute.
Choose every statement that is FALSE.

According to Hobbes, any act that is performed out of fear or intimidation would be an involuntary act.
Pick all the statements that are INCORRECT.

According to Hobbes, it is possible for one to have power even when one lacks the appropriate means to satisfy one's desires.
Pick all INCORRECT statements.

Having a universal desire for power implies that, in Hobbes's state of nature, everybody desires to conquer other people.
According to Hobbes, people with a lot of power can completely avoid getting killed by people with less power.
Which of the following is NOT what Hobbes considers to be a cause of war?

Envy which makes people invade from relative deprivation.
Pick all INCORRECT statements about the moral condition of Hobbes's state of nature.

In the state of nature, everybody is bound by the moral duties prescribed by the laws of nature.
Choose all statements that are INCORRECT.

According to Hobbes, there is no limit to what sort of natural right one can lay aside.
The second law of nature requires each person to cooperate with each other by laying aside their natural rights unconditionally.
Pick every INCORRECT statement.

According to Hobbes, the only way two people can enter into a contract relationship is by explicit consent (i.e. promise.)
According to Hobbes, a contract is binding even when there is nobody to enforce contractual obligations.
Pick every INCORRECT statement about "the fool."

According to the Fool, breaking a covenant is always irrational.
Choose every INCORRECT statement of Hobbes's notion of the commonwealth (i.e. political power/government.)

Among the three types of commonwealth - viz. a monarchy, an aristocracy, and a democracy - Hobbes thought that a democracy is the best form of government.
According to Hobbes, the main problem of a monarchy is the identification of private and public interest.
According to Hobbes, a government that is established by force (i.e. a commonwealth by acquisition) is illegitimate.
Choose all INCORRECT statements about Hobbes's notion of liberty & freedom of subjects.

According to Hobbes, obeying the sovereign's commands out of fear cannot be regarded as a free action.
According to Hobbes, liberty/freedom and fear are mutually incompatible.
According to Hobbes, it is the sovereign's duty to keep the contract that it made with its subjects.
Since the sovereign has absolute powers, the subjects have a moral duty to obey the sovereign's orders even when the sovereign orders a subject to kill him/herself.
